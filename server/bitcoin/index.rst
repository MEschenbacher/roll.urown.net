Bitcoin
=======

.. image:: Bitcoin-logo.*
    :alt: Bitcoin Logo
    :align: right

`Bitcoin <https://bitcoin.org/en/>`_ uses peer-to-peer technology to operate
with no central authority or banks; managing transactions and the issuing of
bitcoins is carried out collectively by the network.
