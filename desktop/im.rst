Instant Messaging

Pidging
-------

.. image:: Pidgin-logo.*
    :alt: XCA Logo
    :align: right

`Pidgin <https://pidgin.im>`_ is an easy to use and free chat client used by
millions. Connect to AIM, MSN, Yahoo, and more chat networks all at once.
